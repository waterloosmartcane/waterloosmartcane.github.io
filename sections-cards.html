Mechanical Subsystem

Parts include the enclosure for the sensor and battery and one for the handle and vibration motor. An omnidirectional wheel is also needed for locomotion.

Parts
  • Enclosure 1
    • Sensor, Battery, Microcontroller Enclosure

      This includes the Jetson Nano, Raspberry Pi, and battery. It has stacking boards with supporting brackets.

    • Technical Specifications

      Measurements are in depth x width x height. Jetson Nano: 100mm x 79mm x 30.21mm Raspberry Pi: 85mm x 56mm x 17mm Battery: 132 mm x 65.5 mm x 9 mm

  • Enclosure 2
    • Vibration Motor Enclosure

      Encloses vibration motor for haptic feedback. Dimensions of 4cm by 1cm by 1cm.

    • Technical Specifications

      Total weight is 1.962N.

  • Cane
    • Normal White Cane

      A traditional white cane will be used as a foundation to add the mechanical and electrical sub systems.

    • Technical Specifications

      The overall cane thickness is 3.5", the overall cane length is 45.75" the overall cane folded length is 13.7", the primary cane material is aluminum, the cane weight is 0.33 kg, the normal force is 11.34N, the static friction is 7.94N, and the torque is 0.2382 mNm

  • Wheel
    • Omnidirectional Wheel

      An omnidirectional wheel allows ease of movement as well as an automated directional change to indicate an updated route to avoid obstacles detected.

    • Technical Specifications

      These are 60mm double aluminum omniwheel basic of type 14145
